About Us

We have been importing Toys, Playing Equipment, School Toys, Gift & Novelties from all over the world and whole selling in India since 1992. We import from China, Taiwan, Israel, France etc. We are also play school toys and play houses Suppliers. 
We entered into retailing in the year 1997. We opened the “Largest Toy Store in Delhi” in Karol Bagh. The showroom exhibits a collection of more than 10,000 items of different varieties of international quality from all over the world. The store covers an area of 5000 sq. ft. and has three storey building. We specialized in Baby products, Toys & Games, Birthday Decoration, Return Gifts, Play School Equipments, Swimming pools, Trampolines, Study Tables, Slide & Swing, Bicycles and Gift Novelties.The Showroom exhibits about 100 Indian and International brands. The motto of our store is to be a one stop destination for parents all over Delhi providing everything for babies and children under one roof.
